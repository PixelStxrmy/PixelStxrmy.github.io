Who am I?

Hi! I'm Stormy — you’ll usually find me somewhere between a sketchpad and a terminal window. I’m an artist by night, a programmer by morning, and occasionally a functional human in between (who needs sleep anyway?).

I make video games not just because I love playing them, but because I think they’re one of the most powerful ways to tell stories — even with simple graphics, they can leave a lasting impact. My goal is to create games that feel alive, emotional, and a little bit weird (in the best way).

What can I do?

I've got a variety of skills built up including, but not limited to:

Programming.
(Godot/GDscript,HTML/CSS/JS,PHP,SQL)

Art.
(Digital and pencil drawing, Pixelart.)

Writing.
(Poetry, light storytelling, character&world building)

Why I do this.

I’ve always been drawn to stories.
The kind that stick with you long after the screen goes dark. Whether it’s through a sketch, a line of code, or a strange little game,
I want to build things that make people feel something Like I did.
Sometimes it may be happiness, other times it may be a deep sorrow. I sincerely hope to create something impactful someday.
